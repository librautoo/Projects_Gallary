Project: Titanic-ML-Competition

Objective

    Find out the potential survivors from the test file given trained model.

Challenge(s) and Solution(s)

    The only challenge I have met is about the feature engineering. What is the strategy to deal with the passenger name and of course # of siblings / spouses, # of parents / children aboard the Titanic
